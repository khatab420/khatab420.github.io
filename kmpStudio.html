1. Information We Collect

We collect certain information automatically when you use the App. This is primarily done through third‑party advertising services (Google AdMob) to provide and improve ads. The types of data collected include:

  • Location: General location (country/region) derived from IP address (not precise GPS).

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., through support inquiries).

AdMob may combine this data with other information they have about you (e.g., from other apps or websites) to serve targeted advertisements. You can manage your ad preferences by visiting Google Ads Settings.
