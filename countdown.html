Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2025 Last updated: July 2026

Thank you for using Count Down. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application. We are committed to transparency and to keeping your data private and secure.

🔒 Quick summary
• Zero data collection – we do not collect any personally identifiable information.
• All timer, alarm, and schedule data is stored locally on your device using SQLite.
• No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.
• The only permission used is Alarm Access to set alarms and manage countdowns – and it is used strictly for that purpose.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from users. The app does not request, store, or transmit your name, email, location, contacts, or any personal data. All timer and scheduling data (e.g., countdown names, alarm times, repeat settings) are stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device. This data never leaves your device.

2. Permissions

The app requests only the minimal permissions needed to provide its core functionality. Below is the permission we use and its purpose:

Alarm Access Used to set alarms and manage countdown timers. This allows the app to schedule notifications and ring alarms at the specified time. The permission is used solely for the intended feature – we do not access or store any alarm data outside of your device.

No other permissions (e.g., camera, location, contacts, storage) are requested or used. The app does not run background services except for alarm scheduling, which is handled by the Android system.

3. Data storage & security

All data – saved timers, alarms, countdown schedules, and preferences – is stored locally on your device using a SQLite database. We do not sync, backup, or upload any data to external servers or cloud services. Since all data remains on your device, you have full control over it. You can clear all app data at any time via your device settings (Settings → Apps → Count Down → Storage → Clear Data) or by uninstalling the app.

4. Third‑party services

The app does not integrate with any third‑party services for data collection, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising. We do not use any SDKs that track user behaviour or share data with external platforms. The app is self‑contained and does not communicate with any external servers.

5. Children’s privacy

Count Down is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. As we collect no data at all, there is no risk of unintentional data collection from minors.

6. Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Effective Date” at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or via the Google Play listing.
