Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026  ·  for version 2.4+

Your privacy matters to us. This privacy policy explains how the پښتو انګلیسي ډکشنري (Pashto‑English Dictionary) app (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles your information. We believe in transparency — and we want you to know exactly what data is collected, why, and how it is used.

📌 Quick summary
• We do not collect, store, or share any personal data you enter (words, translations, notes, or flashcards).
• We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and anonymized usage data to show relevant advertisements.
• We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or any third‑party SDK that tracks your behaviour — except for AdMob.
• Camera permission is used only for OCR / image‑to‑text translation, and images are processed locally on your device; they are never uploaded.

1. Information we collect

No personal data is collected by the app itself. You can use all features — dictionary lookup, flashcards, quizzes, vocabulary notes, and image translation — without creating an account or providing any personal information.

However, because we show advertisements through Google AdMob, certain non‑personal data is automatically collected by Google’s advertising network. This is standard for most free apps that include ads.

Data collected via AdMob (Google)

  • IP address – used to determine your general region (country/city) for ad targeting and frequency capping.
  • Device identifiers – such as Android Advertising ID (AAID) or other anonymized identifiers to deliver relevant ads and prevent fraud.
  • Ad interaction data – impressions, clicks, and other standard engagement metrics so advertisers can measure performance.
  • Device & network information – device model, OS version, carrier, language, and screen size (for ad formatting).

All data collected by AdMob is handled in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy. We do not receive any personally identifiable information (PII) from these interactions — only aggregated or anonymized reports.

2. How we use your information

  • Ad personalization: AdMob uses the data mentioned above to show ads that are more likely to be relevant to you. You can opt out of interest‑based advertising at any time via your device’s Google settings (Ads → Reset / opt out).
  • App functionality: No data is used for any purpose other than serving ads. Your saved vocabulary, notes, flashcards, and quiz progress are stored locally on your device only — never sent to us or any third party.
  • Camera / OCR: When you use the image‑to‑text translation feature, the camera capture is processed directly on your device using on‑device OCR. No image or text is transmitted to our servers or any external service.

3. Third‑party services

We integrate solely with Google AdMob (for advertising). AdMob is a service provided by Google LLC. Their data handling practices are governed by Google’s privacy policies. We do not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or social media trackers.

  • Google AdMob – Google Privacy Policy
  • FileProvider & UCrop – used internally for image cropping; no data is shared externally.

4. Permissions explained

Our manifest includes the following permissions — here is why they are used:

  • INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – required to load ads from AdMob and to check network connectivity.
  • CAMERA – used exclusively for the image‑to‑text (OCR) feature. The camera is only activated when you explicitly choose to capture a picture for translation. No images or data are stored or shared.
  • AD_ID – standard permission for accessing the Android Advertising ID (AAID) used by AdMob for ad personalization.

All permissions are requested at runtime (Android 6+) and are optional — you can still use the dictionary, flashcards, and quizzes without granting camera permissions.

5. Data storage & security

All app‑generated data (vocabulary notes, flashcard decks, quiz scores, saved translations) is stored locally on your device’s internal storage. We do not have access to this data, nor do we back it up to any remote server. To protect your device, we recommend using your device’s built‑in security features (screen lock, encryption).

AdMob data is transmitted over HTTPS and handled according to Google’s security standards.

6. Your choices & control

  • Opt out of ad personalization: On your Android device, go to Settings → Google → Ads and enable “Opt out of Ads Personalization” or reset your advertising ID.
  • Limit ad tracking: You can also limit ad tracking via your device’s privacy settings.
  • Permissions: You may revoke camera permission at any time via your device’s app settings.
  • Data deletion: Since all your personal vocabulary, notes, and flashcards are stored locally, you can delete them by clearing the app’s data (Settings → Apps → پښتو انګلیسي ډکشنري → Storage → Clear Data) or by uninstalling the app.

7. Children’s privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. AdMob may use an age‑screening parameter to serve non‑personalized ads to users under the age of consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via the app or Google Play listing.
