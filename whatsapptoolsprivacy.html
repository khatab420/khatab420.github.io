Privacy Policy for WhatsApp Tools

Package Name: com.lighteast.whatsapptools

Developer Email: esmatullahsangary@gmail.com

WhatsApp Tools ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Android application. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. This policy complies with Google Play's User Data policy requirements.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Data Collection

WhatsApp Tools does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All operations happen locally on your device. We have no servers, no databases, and no analytics that track your behavior.

1.2 What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ No personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • ❌ No device information (IMEI, MAC address, serial numbers)
  • ❌ No location data (GPS or network location)
  • ❌ No usage statistics or analytics
  • ❌ No crash logs (unless you voluntarily share them via email)
  • ❌ No contacts or address book data
  • ❌ No WhatsApp messages or status content (all processing is local)

2. Feature-Specific Data Handling

2.1 Status Saver Feature

The Status Saver feature works entirely offline on your device:

  • Access: The app requests storage permission to read WhatsApp's status folder and save content to your Downloads folder.
  • What it reads: Images and videos from /WhatsApp/Media/.Statuses/ and /Android/media/com.whatsapp/WhatsApp/Media/.Statuses/
  • What it saves: Files are copied to Downloads/WhatsAppTools/Statuses/ on your device
  • No upload: Status files are NEVER uploaded to any server or cloud
  • No sharing: The app does not share your status content with any third party
  • Ownership: Saved statuses remain under your control on your device

2.2 Auto Reply Feature

  • Notification Access: With your explicit permission, the app listens for WhatsApp notifications to trigger auto-replies
  • Local processing: All message matching and replies are processed locally on your device
  • No storage: We do not store or log your messages or conversations
  • RemoteInput API: Replies are sent using Android's standard RemoteInput API through WhatsApp's notification actions
  • No message content access: The app only sees notification content to trigger rules, not your full chat history

2.3 Message Scheduler

  • Local storage: Scheduled messages are stored locally in your device's Room database
  • No cloud sync: Messages are never backed up to any external server
  • Deep linking: The app uses wa.me deep links to open WhatsApp with pre-filled messages
  • Manual sending: Messages are NOT sent automatically; you must press send in WhatsApp
  • Data deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all scheduled messages

2.4 Direct Chat Feature

  • Opens WhatsApp using public https://wa.me/ deep links
  • No phone numbers or messages are stored permanently
  • You manually type or select the contact information

3. Permissions Explained

We request the following permissions to provide core functionality. Each permission is accompanied by a system prompt explaining why it's needed.

3.1 Storage Permissions (Android 12 and below)

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO: Required to scan WhatsApp's status folder and display available statuses for you to save.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Required to save selected statuses to your Downloads/WhatsAppTools/Statuses/ folder.

3.2 Notification Permissions

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): Required to show app notifications (e.g., "Status saved successfully", "Auto reply triggered").
  • Notification Listener Service: With your explicit consent, enables the Auto Reply feature to detect incoming WhatsApp messages.

3.3 Other Permissions

  • INTERNET: Used only for loading URLs (opening WhatsApp links) and displaying AdMob ads (if implemented). No user data is transmitted.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Restores your scheduled messages after device restart.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM: Precisely triggers scheduled messages at your specified time.
  • VIBRATE: Provides haptic feedback when auto-reply is triggered (optional).

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Advertising (if applicable)

WhatsApp Tools may display Google AdMob ads to keep the app free. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising ID (resettable by user)
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address, not GPS)

AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

4.2 No Other Third Parties

We do not use any other third-party SDKs, analytics tools (Firebase, Facebook Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.), or crash reporting tools (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.).

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local-Only Storage

  • Room Database: Stores your auto-reply rules and scheduled messages. Located at: /data/data/com.lighteast.whatsapptools/databases/
  • DataStore Preferences: Stores app settings (e.g., throttle delay).
  • Saved Statuses: Stored in /storage/emulated/0/Downloads/WhatsAppTools/Statuses/

5.2 Security Measures

  • All data remains on your device only
  • No data is transmitted over the internet (except AdMob ads if enabled)
  • No remote servers or cloud backups
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all app data

6. Your Rights and Control

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to delete, modify, or export. However, you have full control over:

  • Revoke permissions: Go to Settings → Apps → WhatsApp Tools → Permissions
  • Disable Auto Reply: Turn off notification access in Settings → Notifications → Notification access
  • Delete saved statuses: Use any file manager to delete Downloads/WhatsAppTools/Statuses/
  • Clear app data: Settings → Apps → WhatsApp Tools → Storage & cache → Clear storage
  • Uninstall: Complete removal of all app data

7. Children's Privacy

WhatsApp Tools does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app is not directed at children, and the Status Saver feature is designed for users who have permission to access WhatsApp content on their device. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us.

8. Data Deletion Requests

To delete all data associated with WhatsApp Tools:

  1. Uninstall the app from your device
  2. OR go to Settings → Apps → WhatsApp Tools → Storage → Clear Data
  3. To delete saved statuses, manually delete: Downloads/WhatsAppTools/Statuses/ folder

Since no data is stored on external servers, no further action is required from us.
